404 Not Found

This 404 was generated by the ddev-webserver container (Apache), not by your project's application or CMS code.

This usually means one of the following:
  - The requested file does not exist.
  - The 'docroot' setting in your project's .ddev/config.yaml does not point to the right directory.
  - php-fpm could not find the PHP script it was asked to run.

Things to check:
  - Confirm 'docroot' in .ddev/config.yaml matches where your project's index.php actually lives.
  - Confirm the requested file exists at that path.
  - Run 'ddev logs' to check for php-fpm errors.
