404: Not Found

This 404 was generated by the ddev-webserver container, not by your project's application or CMS code. This is not your project's own 404 page. This usually means the requested file does not exist, or the docroot setting in your project's .ddev/config.yaml does not point to the right directory.
  1. Confirm docroot in .ddev/config.yaml matches where your project's index.php actually lives.
  2. Confirm the requested file exists at that path.
  3. Run ddev logs to check for php-fpm errors.
